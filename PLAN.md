# Rework Reflect cards into yes/no prompts with auto-journal + editable save

## What's changing

### Reflection cards (the swipe deck)

- Replace the current statement cards (things like "You admitted you were wrong") with gentle, open **yes/no prompts** — e.g. "Did you show up today?", "Were you kind to yourself?", "Did you make a little progress?", "Did you rest when you needed to?"
- Tone is always warm, encouraging, and non-judgmental — no prompts that imply fault or self-criticism
- Swipe right = Yes, swipe left = No (no guilt for "No" answers — they still count as reflection)

### Ring closing

- The Reflect ring now closes as soon as you finish the day's prompts — whether you swiped Yes or No on each one. Simply showing up and reflecting completes the ring.

### AI-written journal entry

- After the last card, the app automatically generates a warm, first-person journal entry based on your Yes/No answers
- The AI is instructed to be happy, encouraging, and judgment-free — it celebrates the Yeses and frames the Nos as gentle self-awareness (never criticism)
- The entry appears inline in an editable text box
- You can tweak the wording, then tap **Save to Journal** to log it
- A "Regenerate" option is available if you want a different version

### Screens affected

- **Today screen**: new prompt-style cards, Yes/No labels instead of "Earned/Skip"
- **Journal / Reflect section**: editable AI entry with Save button; once saved, it shows as your journal for the day and the Reflect ring stays closed
